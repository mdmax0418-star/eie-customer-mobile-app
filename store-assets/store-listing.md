# EIE Customer Store Listing Draft

## App name
EIE Customer

## Subtitle / short description
Submit auto registration service requests and documents securely.

## Full description
EIE Customer makes it simple to start and manage auto registration service requests from your phone. Sign in to the EIE Customer Portal, choose the service you need, add your contact information, and upload supporting documents such as PDFs or images.

Use EIE Customer for common registration needs including registration renewals, title transfers, replacement plates or stickers, VIN verification, and out-of-state transfers. The app helps customers send complete information to EIE Auto Registration so requests can be reviewed faster and followed up on by the service team.

Key features:
- Secure customer portal sign-in
- New service request submission
- Document upload for PDFs and images
- Support for up to 5 documents per request
- Admin dashboard access for authorized staff
- Status tracking and document review tools for staff users

EIE Customer is intended for EIE Auto Registration customers and authorized staff. Service availability, processing times, and document requirements may vary by request type.
