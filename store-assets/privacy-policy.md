# Privacy Policy for EIE Customer

Effective date: June 12, 2026

EIE Customer is provided for customers and authorized staff of EIE Auto Registration. This policy explains what information the mobile app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

## Information we collect

When you use the app, we may collect:

- Account information used to sign in, such as username and authentication status.
- Service request information, such as full name, phone number, selected service type, request status, and submission date.
- Documents you choose to upload, including PDFs and image files that may contain vehicle, identity, registration, or other supporting information.
- Technical information needed to operate the service, such as API requests, security logs, and basic device/network diagnostics.

The app does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not sell personal information.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Create, review, and process auto registration service requests.
- Contact customers about submitted requests.
- Allow authorized staff to review submissions, documents, and request statuses.
- Maintain app security, prevent unauthorized access, and troubleshoot service issues.
- Comply with legal, regulatory, or business record requirements.

## Document uploads

Users choose which files to upload. Uploaded documents are transmitted to EIE Auto Registration's backend service and are intended only for request processing and staff review. Do not upload documents that are not needed for your service request.

## Sharing information

Information may be shared with EIE Auto Registration staff, service providers that host or support the app, and government or regulatory entities when needed to complete requested services or comply with law. We do not share information with advertisers.

## Data retention

Service request records and documents are retained for as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. EIE Auto Registration may delete or archive records when they are no longer required.

## Security

The app uses HTTPS to communicate with the backend service and stores authentication tokens using platform-secure storage where available. No security method is perfect, so users should protect their login credentials and report suspected unauthorized access promptly.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for vehicle registration service customers and authorized staff.

## Your choices

You may choose not to submit a request or not to upload optional documents. To request access, correction, or deletion of information, contact EIE Auto Registration using the support contact below.
